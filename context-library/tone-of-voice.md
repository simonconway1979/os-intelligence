# Tone of Voice

This is a stub. The default writing-style guides live in [`writing-styles-global/`](writing-styles-global/) and cover five audiences: customer, executive, internal, social, technical.

**Edit them to your tone.** They were drafted as defaults so the `ctx-` and content skills have something to anchor on. Replace the examples, swap the phrasing, drop the bits you don't agree with. Anything you write here or in `writing-styles-global/` will be picked up next time a skill needs a tone reference.

If you have an existing brand voice document, paste it in. If you don't, the defaults will give you a starting point that's worth iterating on once you see what the skills produce.
