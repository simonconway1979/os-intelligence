# Executive Writing Style

Use for board updates, leadership communication, strategy docs.

## Characteristics

**Tone:** Strategic, concise, data-driven
**Audience:** Executives, board members, investors
**Goal:** Drive decisions with clear recommendations

## Rules

### 1. Lead with Bottom Line
Start with the answer, then explain.

- Avoid: "We tested 3 approaches over 6 weeks. First we tried..."
- Better: "Recommendation: Invest in Solution A. Expected ROI: [X]%. Details below."

### 2. Be Concise
Executives are busy. Get to the point.
- 1 page > 3 pages
- Bullets > paragraphs
- Data > adjectives
- Action > analysis

### 3. Quantify Everything
- Avoid: "Sales are improving"
- Better: "Sales up [X]% QoQ to $[Y] ARR"

- Avoid: "Users love the feature"
- Better: "NPS increased from [X] to [Y] post-launch"

### 4. Focus on Business Impact
Connect everything to:
- Revenue
- Growth
- Market position
- Strategic goals
- Risk

### 5. Provide Clear Options
Format: Option A, B, C with pros/cons, then YOUR recommendation.

## Structure

**1. Executive Summary (3-5 bullets)**
- Current situation
- Key insight
- Recommendation
- Impact
- Next steps

**2. Context (Brief)**
- Why this matters now
- What changed

**3. Analysis**
- Data and evidence
- Options considered
- Trade-offs

**4. Recommendation**
- Clear choice
- Rationale
- Expected outcomes
- Resource requirements

**5. Next Steps**
- Who does what by when

## Example

**Bad:**
```
We've been working on improving user engagement. There are several interesting
approaches we could take. Let me walk you through our thinking...

[3 pages of detailed analysis]
```

**Good:**
```
## Recommendation: [Clear Action]

**Impact:** +[X]% [metric] -> +$[Y] ARR
**Cost:** $[Z] ([timeline], [resources])
**ROI:** [X]% in first year

**Context:**
Current [metric]: [value] (vs. [benchmark])
Root cause: [Brief explanation]

**Options Evaluated:**
A. [Option A]: [Impact], [Cost] [Recommended marker]
B. [Option B]: [Impact], [Cost]
C. [Option C]: [Impact], [Cost]

**Risk:** [Primary risk]
Mitigation: [How to address it]

**Timeline:**
- MVP: [Duration]
- Full rollout: [Duration]
- Break-even: [Duration]

**Next Steps:**
- [Step 1]: [Date]
- [Step 2]: [Date]
- [Step 3]: [Cadence]
```

## When to Use
- Board presentations
- QBRs
- Strategy documents
- Leadership updates
- Investment proposals
