# Strategy — Acme Corp

## North Star: AI-First by 2026

**Public commitment:** Every meaningful step of the customer hiring workflow will be AI-enhanced by end of 2026.

**Announced:**
- Press release 2026-01-03 (picked up by HR Executive, TechCrunch Europe, Sunday Times Tech Track)
- Internal CEO memo 2026-01-04
- Confirmed in Q1 board update 2026-04-06 and Series C investor update 2026-03-02
- On the public website + Series C investor deck

**Naming inconsistency to manage:** The public brand is sometimes labelled "AI-First by 2025" (announcement year) but delivery target is end of 2026. Section headers in some assets carry the older naming. Worth a copy-pass for consistency before any external moment.

**Stated rationale (CEO):**
- Partly defensive — six of top 30 accounts named a specific competitor in 2025 sales cycles; two renewals had AI feature dates as conditions
- Partly ambitious — be the reference brand for AI in enterprise hiring
- Forcing function to ship

## Three AI Pillars

1. **AI candidate matching** (flagship)
   - Embedding-based semantic match against ~18 months of Acme Corp hiring decision data
   - Public demo: HR Tech Reimagined Europe, ExCeL London, **9 August 2026** (~600 attendees, CEO keynote)
   - Production target: live with at least one design-partner enterprise customer by end of Q2 2026
   - Detail in `product.md`, `roadmap.md`, `events.md`

2. **Workflow intelligence** (AI screening, smart scheduling, automated reference checks)
   - Beta target Q3 2026 (per strategy deck) / Q1 2027 (per Q1 board update — investigate which is current)
   - PRD ownership floating as of April 2026 — Simon to resolve in Month 1

3. **Candidate experience** (personalised AI feedback, improved rejection/offer letters)
   - End-of-2026 target
   - No discovery work started — Simon to decide whether to start now or post-Q2 launch

## What the Commitment Includes

- Bias audit and security review required before any AI feature launch (non-negotiable per CEO memo, but no owner / process / timeline assigned)
- Engineering leaders (Wei Chen, Tom Okafor, Kasia Nowak) expected in sales conversations requiring AI roadmap commitments
- Customer data NOT used to train models for other customers (explicit commitment)
- Every AI decision will be explainable — no black-box candidate decisions
- AI included in core pricing for 2026 — no price uplift

## What It Excludes / Doesn't Address

- Foundation models only — Acme builds on top, not training from scratch (no dedicated AI/ML hiring planned)
- Platform stability investment continues alongside AI work — Johnny explicitly committed not to trade one for the other
- See `governance.md` for the AI policy gaps that the commitment leaves unresolved

## North America

Flagged as the next strategic wave **if 2026 delivers**.
