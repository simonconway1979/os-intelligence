## Structural Notes / Tensions

1. **No dedicated AI org line.** AI/ML sits inside Kasia Nowak's Applied Research squad in Warsaw engineering. No exec-level Head of AI or Head of Data & ML. Simon's "Head of Product — AI" title has no engineering counterpart at VP level. The CEO's AI-First commitment has no matching org design.

2. **Customer Success structurally isolated** from Product and Revenue — reports to COO Sarah Mensah. Customer-pain-to-product-fix loop is informal. Simon must build the James Whitfield relationship proactively.

3. **Marketing fragmentation:** No CMO. Diego Ferreira (VP Marketing) reports direct to CEO. Patrick O'Sullivan (Head of Product Marketing) sits under Marcus. Potential misalignment between product narrative and market narrative.

4. **80% of engineering is in Warsaw; 100% of product is in London.** Cross-timezone dependency embedded in every AI delivery decision. Standard overlap window: 09:00–13:00 UK / 10:00–14:00 Warsaw.
