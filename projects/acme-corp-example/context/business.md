# Business — Acme Corp

## Company Snapshot

- **Founded:** 2018
- **HQ:** London (commercial, customer success, marketing, finance, most of product)
- **Engineering + AI/ML hub:** Warsaw
- **Headcount:** 180 (target 195 by end FY 2026); ~110 London, ~70 Warsaw
- **Stage:** Series C — £42M closed Q3 2024, led by Crescent Ventures
- **Investors:** Sequoia Capital (Series A lead), Prosus Ventures (Series B lead), Crescent Ventures (Series C lead), Norrsken VC. All four investors signed up to the AI-First commitment.

## Financials (Q1 2026 close)

- **ARR:** £18.0M
- **2026 target:** £24M ARR (33% growth, board high-confidence case)
- **Q1 new ARR:** £1.6M; Q2 forecast £1.4M; FY target £6.0M
- **NRR:** 108% (FY 2025); target ≥110% in 2026
- **Logo retention:** 94% (FY 2025); target ≥95% — **below plan, primary watchpoint**
- **Burn:** £1.05M/month; 36 months runway on Series C

## Customer Base

- **Scale:** 220+ enterprise customers; 4M+ applications processed annually
- **Markets:** UK, Europe, North America (NA expansion is the "next wave" if 2026 delivers)
- **Named customers:** Avlon Insurance, Mercer-Lawson Consulting (active); **Northwind Bank** (churned Q1 2026, £312k ARR — see `customers.md`); **Hartfield Retail Group** (churned Q4 2025)

## Product

Enterprise ATS + candidate experience platform for hiring teams moving at scale.

- Applicant tracking (pipeline, scheduling, collaboration, decisions, offers)
- Candidate experience (branded flows, status updates, staged feedback)
- Hiring analytics (funnel, source-of-hire, time-to-fill, diversity)
- Workflow automation (scheduling, reference checks, offer generation)

Detail in `product.md`.
